<p><a href="http://www.lightmark.de/index.htm">Incredible &#8220;light paintings&#8221;.</a></p>

<p>If you have ever seen the Sprint commercials with animations created using flashlights, then you get the concept. These photographs, by Cenci Goepel and Jens Warnecke, however, are far more striking. The landscapes are beautiful; I particularly like the shots in Norway, where you can see the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aurora_Borealis">Aurora Borealis.</a></p>
