<p>Bjarke Ingels Group of Denmark designed these towers, with a goal of breaking away from the typical rectilinear silhouette of most modern buildings. They used a very unique process to develop the form, and then constructed an amazing model entirely out of Legos. The scale of the project, and the detail involved is very impressive.</p>
