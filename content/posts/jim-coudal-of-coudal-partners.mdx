<p>Jim Coudal is a truly inspiring character, with a unique perspective on business and creativity. His company, <a href="http://www.coudal.com/">Coudal Partners</a>, is not only a well respected design agency, but has also developed many successful products and services, most notably <a href="http://decknetwork.net/">The Deck</a> advertising network, as well as <a href="http://fieldnotesbrand.com/">Field Notes</a> notebooks. He often stresses the importance of writing, as well as letting go of the fear of failure.</p>

People always ask, “What is your greatest failure?” I always have the same answer – We’re working on it right now, it’s gonna be awesome!
